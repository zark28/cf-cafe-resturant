The Restaurant

Concept Foods strives to use local, sustainable, and organic ingredients. We put a lot of effort into finding the best ingredients, and we make everything from scratch with love. We also try to minimize our environmental impact whenever possible and pay our workforce a living salary .

Overall, these factors result in increased menu pricing, but we hope that you still find them reasonable and that it gives you some peace of mind knowing that we strive to do what is essential to us every day.
